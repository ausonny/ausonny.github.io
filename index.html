Glossary
Inflation:
Anything bought with wood ir stone is affected by inflation. Once you have bought more than the inflation level (which usually starts at 20) then the price begins to increase at a faster rate. The inflation challenge raises the quantity at which inflation begins.

Speed:
1x - Run at normal speed
2x - Run at double speed
5x - Run at 5 times speed
(Speed is always maxed at 50ms per game update.)
P - Pause

Tactics:
F - Target the enemy with the least time remaining on the map
M - Target enemy with most health remaining
L - Target enemy with least health remaining
H - Target enemy healers first then defaults to F tactic

Critical Hits:
If the chance is greater than 100% then you are guranteed at least one critical multiplier. For example if the chance is 110% and the multiplier is 5 then you are guaranteed a multiplier of 5 and have a 10% chance of a multiplier of 25. Blueprints: A blueprint of the current tower layout can be stored. You have one blueprint per tier. A level 2 tier can not be used on tier 1. If blueprints are turned on the tower types will be set automatically with zero levels bought after prestige or any map reset.
