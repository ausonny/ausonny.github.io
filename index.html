Inflation: Anything bought with metal is affected by inflation. Once you have bought more than the inflation level (which usually starts at 20) then the price begins to increase at a faster rate. The inflation challenge raises the quantity at which inflation begins.

Speed: N - Run at normal speed
F - Fast (max) speed (50ms per frame)
P - Pause

Tactics:
F - Target the enemy with the least time remaining on the map
M - Target enemy with most health remaining
L - Target enemy with least health remaining
H - Target enemy healers first then defaults to F tactic
